  • SAN FRANCISCO MARITIME NATIONAL HISTORICAL PARK @ San Francisco

    Timeframe: June 19th to August 11th 2017

    Paid: $14/hr

    Description

    San Francisco Maritime National Historical Park is currently recruiting four youth for the 2017 Youth Conservation Corps (YCC) Program. YCC is a summer employment program aimed at young men and women ages 15 through 18, without regard to social, economic, racial, or ethnic backgrounds. Through work projects in the park, YCC crew gains a better understanding of their environment and management of our cultural resources.

    The YCC Crew will work on a wide variety of projects including: restoration and maintenance of the historic ships at Hyde Street Pier such as: painting, varnishing, and learning basic maritime skills; landscape management projects such as: pruning, weeding, mulching, replanting, renovating ornamental beds, and learning about landscape management in a historical park. They will participate in a wide variety of other projects in other departments as related to a cultural park. Work hours will be 7 am-3:30 PM, Monday through Friday.

    Requirements

    Students must be CITIZENS OR PERMANENT RESIDENTS AGES 15- 18, and must not turn 19 until after 8/11. Each member must provide their own transportation to and from work, a pre-appointment physical exam, minimal uniform items, and a student work permit.
