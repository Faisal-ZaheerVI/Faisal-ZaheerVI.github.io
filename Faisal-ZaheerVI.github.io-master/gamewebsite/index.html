THE STORY

Outbreak! Humankind has finally made First Contact with an unknown, non-human lifeform. Brought in for research by Czerka Corporation, unknown that the alien carries a deadly, infectious parasite, a catastrophe has spread throughout the city! Play as a scientist who has to escape from the chaos and find the alien!

WHAT IS THE GAME?

  • First Contact is a platformer game with an original narrative.
  • We chose this type of game because it goes back to when the game industry boomed with platformer games like Mario and Donkey Kong. Having a narrative enhances the game development experience, allowing for more creativity in making the game.
